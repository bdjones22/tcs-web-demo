Clinician Profile

Bobby Brown III, MA., LPCC

Client-centered, trauma-informed care with a culturally grounded and spiritually rooted perspective.

Specialties: Trauma · Anxiety · Depression · Life Transitions · Spirituality & Identity
Populations: Adults and families · Marginalized and BIPOC communities

Your journey toward healing and strength

Life’s challenges and past traumas can cast a long shadow, especially for those in marginalized communities. If you are ready to move toward a more healthy and functional life, I am here to walk with you. My purpose is to help you overcome the emotional and social impacts of trauma and boldly live the happy, healthy life you deserve.

A compassionate and culturally responsive approach

As a Black male psychotherapist, I offer a therapy space that is deeply respectful of your cultural context and personal history. My approach is client-centered and trauma-informed, built on the belief that you are the expert on your own life. I provide a supportive and collaborative environment, gently guiding you toward deeper insight in one’s own humanity—with a touch of humor, all rooted in spirituality.

Specialized expertise for your growth

Using evidence-based therapeutic interventions, we will work together to develop effective strategies for your healing. My training includes:

  • Client-Centered Therapy: Empowering you to guide your own process toward self-discovery and growth.
  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT): Identifying and reshaping negative thought patterns that may be holding you back.
  • Insight-Oriented Therapy: Exploring how past experiences influence your present emotions and behaviors.
  • Trauma-Informed Interventions and Trauma-Focused CBT: Addressing the lasting effects of trauma in a way that respects your experience and promotes resilience.
  • Motivational Interviewing: Helping you tap into the internal motivation needed to create meaningful change.
  • Mindfulness, Grounding, and Relaxation Techniques: Providing practical tools to manage stress and stay present.
  • Behavioral Management: Equipping you with skills to overcome obstacles and pursue your goals.

My commitment to you

We will work together to value and edify your voice, effectively utilize your unique talents, and achieve the life goals that matter most to you. Your audacity to live a healthy, self-empowered life is the focus of our work together.
