Evidence-based, person-centered care

Our mission is to support your growth, not define you by your pain.

We believe you are more than a diagnosis or a difficult season of life. Our role is to walk alongside you with honesty, warmth, and clinical expertise so you can move toward a more healthy, authentic, and fulfilled life.

At Empowered Living Mental Health Solutions, we work with adults and families navigating anxiety, depression, trauma, substance use, and complex life transitions. We strive to create a respectful, affirming space that honors culture, identity, and lived experience.

What you can expect in working with us:

  • Down-to-earth conversation and practical skills you can use outside of session.
  • Culturally responsive therapy that recognizes the realities our communities face.
  • Trauma-informed care that centers your safety, dignity, and choice.
  • Collaborative treatment planning focused on your values and goals.
  • Supportive accountability as you move toward the life you want to live.

Our Services

We integrate clinical training with real-world understanding to meet you where you are and support where you’re going.

Individual Therapy

One-on-one counseling to address anxiety, depression, trauma, substance use, stress, and life transitions at your own pace.

Couples & Relationship Counseling

Support for couples and partners seeking healthier communication, repair after conflict, and greater connection.

Multicultural & Identity-Affirming Counseling

Therapy that centers culture, race, and identity—honoring the full context of your lived experience.

Teletherapy (Virtual Sessions)

Secure online sessions for clients who prefer or need care from home, work, or on the go.

Rehabilitation & Recovery Support

Integrated support for mental health and substance use challenges, grounded in compassion and accountability.

Cognitive & Supportive Interventions

Skills-based and insight-oriented work to help you better understand patterns and build healthier ways of coping.
